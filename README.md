# 🚦 GRIDLOCK AI

### AI-Powered Parking Intelligence & Congestion Analytics Platform

GRIDLOCK AI is an intelligent urban traffic management platform developed for the Flipkart GridLock Hackathon. The solution leverages data analytics, visualization, and AI-driven insights to identify illegal parking hotspots, quantify their impact on traffic congestion, and support data-driven enforcement planning.

## 📌 Problem Statement

Urban roads near commercial hubs, metro stations, educational institutions, and public events often experience severe congestion due to illegal and spillover parking. Current enforcement mechanisms are largely reactive and patrol-based, making it difficult to identify high-impact violation zones and allocate resources efficiently.

### Key Challenges

* Lack of real-time visibility into parking-induced congestion.
* No prioritization framework for enforcement teams.
* Limited understanding of the relationship between parking violations and traffic flow disruption.
* Inefficient allocation of enforcement resources.

---

## 💡 Our Solution

GRIDLOCK AI transforms raw parking violation data into actionable intelligence through an interactive command center dashboard.

The platform helps authorities:

* Detect high-risk parking violation hotspots.
* Analyze congestion impact across zones and intersections.
* Monitor parking trends over time.
* Identify enforcement priority areas.
* Support proactive traffic management decisions.

---

## 🚀 Features

### 🎯 Intelligent Dashboard

A centralized command center providing real-time insights into parking violations and congestion metrics.

### 🗺️ Hotspot Detection

Identify locations with the highest concentration of parking violations using spatial analytics.

### 📈 Trend Analysis

Analyze violation patterns across different time periods to understand recurring congestion issues.

### 🏢 Zone Intelligence

Evaluate traffic and parking behavior across different city zones and enforcement regions.

### 🚔 Enforcement Planning

Generate data-backed recommendations for deployment of enforcement resources.

### 📊 Congestion Impact Scoring

Measure how parking violations influence traffic flow and roadway performance.
